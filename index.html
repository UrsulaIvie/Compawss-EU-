Compawss

🧡 For Adopters: Find the Right Soul, Not Just a Pet

Compawss is your companion on the journey to adoption. We connect you to all adoptable animals listed across shelters in your country — or, in the case of the European Union, from shelters in every participating country.

🤝 For Volunteers: Help Where It’s Needed Most

With Compawss, you can see all the shelters and organizations currently seeking help, both in your country and across borders.

📅 For Shelters: Share Events, Reach More People

Compawss solves that with a built-in AI event booster: just enter your event and we’ll help share it across communities and social platforms.

🐾 For Everyone: A Mission of Animal Welfare

We support shelters, adopters, rescuers, volunteers, and communities with tools to reduce overcrowding and reunite lost animals.
