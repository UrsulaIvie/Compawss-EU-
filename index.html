About Compawss

Compawss is an AI-powered platform that connects shelters, adopters, and volunteers for all animals — from dogs and cats to snakes, rabbits, birds, and hamsters. We use intelligent matchmaking, event boosting, and community engagement tools to improve adoption success and shelter efficiency. Everyone deserves a forever home — and every species deserves visibility.
