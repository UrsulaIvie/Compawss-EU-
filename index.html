About Compawss

Compawss is an AI-driven platform built to improve animal adoption outcomes, volunteer organization, and event visibility for shelters of all kinds. Whether you're a rabbit lover, bird rescuer, or looking to adopt a senior snake, Compawss is designed for you. Our mission is to make pet adoption inclusive, smart, and truly compassionate.
