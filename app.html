The Compawss App is Coming Soon!

We're building something amazing — and you're part of it!

Our goal is to use Artificial Intelligence to find the ideal home for each pet, and the ideal pet for each home. The Compawss app will make the adoption process easier, more informed, and more compassionate.

With it, we will:

  • create ideal matches between pets and humans,
  • digitally streamline the adoption process,
  • reduce returns through better-informed decisions.

This is just the beginning. We aim to build a community, use data ethically, and transform animal welfare through empathy and innovation.

Stay tuned — the future of Compawss is just getting started!
