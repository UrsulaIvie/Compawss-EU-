Partnerships

At Compawss, we believe that true impact is born from collaboration. That’s why we are always seeking partnerships with shelters, NGOs, companies, academic institutions, and community initiatives that share our purpose: to promote animal welfare and make adoption more accessible, safe, and compassionate.

If you represent an organization that wants to join us on this journey, get in touch! Together we can:

  • Increase the visibility of animals available for adoption;
  • Promote adoption events and fairs;
  • Share volunteer opportunities;
  • Gather data for future tech solutions, such as our upcoming app;
  • Educate the public about responsible pet ownership and animal protection.

Let’s build an ecosystem of animal care — together!
