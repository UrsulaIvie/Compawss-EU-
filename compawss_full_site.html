About Compawss

Compawss is an AI-driven platform designed to improve outcomes for pet adoptions, optimize rescue operations, increase visibility for underrepresented animals (like snakes and rodents), and connect passionate volunteers with shelters. Whether you're seeking a furry friend, a feathered pal, or a scaled companion, Compawss is your guide.

What Compawss Does

  • Smart AI Matching Between Humans and Animals
  • Optimized Rescue and Emergency Coordination
  • Volunteer Matching and Scheduling Tools
  • Pet Adoption Events & Awareness Boost
  • Shelter Analytics & Dashboard Management
  • Inclusivity: Cats, Dogs, Reptiles, Rodents & More
